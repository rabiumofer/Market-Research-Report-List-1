<p><p>The chalk marker market is segmented primarily into two types based on nib design: bullet nib and chisel nib markers. In contrast, chisel nib markers have a flat, angled tip that enables broader strokes and varied line thickness, making them ideal for bold lettering and emphasizing text. Both types cater to different artistic and practical applications, appealing to various consumer preferences.</p></p>
