<p><p>The Composite Backsheet market is segmented into two main types: Fluoropolymer and Non-Fluoropolymer. In contrast, Non-Fluoropolymer backsheets offer cost-effectiveness and are typically made from alternative materials like polyethylene or polyester, catering to a broader range of applications.
