<p><strong>The Disinfectants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidising Disinfectants</li><li>Non-oxidising Disinfectants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disinfectants market is primarily categorized into oxidizing and non-oxidizing disinfectants. Oxidizing disinfectants, such as chlorine and hydrogen peroxide, work by releasing reactive oxygen species that destroy microbial cell walls and proteins. In contrast, non-oxidizing disinfectants, like quaternary ammonium compounds and phenolic compounds, eliminate pathogens through different mechanisms, such as disrupting cell membranes. Both types play essential roles in various settings, including healthcare, food processing, and water treatment, ensuring sanitation and safety against microbial threats.</p></p>
