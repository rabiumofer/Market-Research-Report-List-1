<p><strong>The ETFE Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Coating</li><li>Fluid Dipping Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ETFE coatings, known for their durability and chemical resistance, are applied using methods like powder coating and fluid dipping. Powder coating involves electrostatically applying dry ETFE powder to surfaces, which is then cured to form a tough, uniform layer. Fluid dipping coating submerges objects into liquid ETFE, creating a seamless, protective film after drying. Both methods enhance corrosion resistance, thermal stability, and non-stick properties, making them ideal for industries like automotive, construction, and chemical processing.</p></p>
