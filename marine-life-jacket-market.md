<p><strong>The Marine Life Jacket Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buoyant Material Filled Life Jacket</li><li>Inflatable Life Jacket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine life jacket market primarily includes two types: buoyant material filled life jackets and inflatable life jackets. Buoyant material filled life jackets are constructed from foam or similar materials, offering inherent buoyancy and immediate flotation without requiring inflation. They are durable and suitable for various water conditions. In contrast, inflatable life jackets are compact and lighter, providing buoyancy when inflated, making them popular for recreational activities. Both types cater to safety needs while differing in design, comfort, and performance characteristics.</p></p>
