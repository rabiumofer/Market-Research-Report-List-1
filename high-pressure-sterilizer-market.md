<p><p>The high pressure sterilizer market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal high pressure sterilizers. Vertical high pressure sterilizers are designed with a vertical chamber, allowing for a compact footprint, suitable for limited spaces. They often accommodate smaller loads and are popular in laboratories. In contrast, horizontal high pressure sterilizers feature a horizontal chamber, providing larger capacity for bulk sterilization, commonly used in hospitals and pharmaceutical industries for larger equipment and materials. Both serve critical roles in ensuring effective sterilization.</p></p>
