<p><p>Non-toluene ink for flexible packaging includes various solvent types, primarily ethyl acetate and n-butyl acetate. Ethyl acetate is favored for its rapid drying properties and lower odor, making it well-suited for high-speed printing applications. N-butyl acetate offers excellent adhesion and flexibility, contributing to durability and resistance in flexible packaging. Both solvents provide effective alternatives to toluene-based inks, aligning with environmental regulations while maintaining print quality, making them increasingly popular in the packaging industry.</p></p>
