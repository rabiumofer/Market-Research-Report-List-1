<p><p>The commercial masticating juicer market features two primary types: vertical auger and horizontal auger juicers. Vertical auger commercial masticating juicers operate with a vertically positioned auger that efficiently extracts juice while minimizing oxidation, making them ideal for leafy greens and softer fruits. In contrast, horizontal auger commercial masticating juicers utilize a horizontal design, offering versatility for various ingredients, including hard fruits and vegetables. Both types are designed for high efficiency and durability, catering to the demands of commercial juice production.</p></p>
