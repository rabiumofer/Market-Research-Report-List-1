<p><p>The Infrared Fluorescent Ink market encompasses three primary types: Infrared Excited Ink, which fluoresces when exposed to infrared light, enhancing security features and readability; Infrared Absorption Ink, which absorbs infrared light, useful for camouflage and stealth applications; and Infrared Covering Ink, designed to obscure underlying images or text when viewed without infrared illumination. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries, including packaging, security printing, and specialty applications, leveraging unique properties of infrared technology for functional and aesthetic benefits.</p></p>
