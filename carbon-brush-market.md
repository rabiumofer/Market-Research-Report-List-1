<p><p>The carbon brush market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Electrographite brushes are known for their high conductivity and thermal resistance, ideal for electric motors. Graphite brushes offer good wear resistance, suitable for low-speed applications. Metal graphite brushes combine graphite with metal particles, enhancing conductivity and durability, making them effective in heavy-duty environments. Silver graphite brushes, which incorporate silver for improved conductivity, are used in high-performance applications, particularly where efficient electrical contact is essential. Each type caters to specific operational needs.</p></p>
