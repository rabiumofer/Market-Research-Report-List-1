<p><p>The ceramic ultrafiltration membrane market consists of two primary types: flat-sheet membranes and pipe membranes. Flat-sheet membranes are widely used in various filtration applications due to their efficient surface area and ease of integration into existing systems. They offer high flux rates and durability. Pipe membranes, on the other hand, are cylindrical in shape and are typically utilized in larger-scale operations, such as water treatment plants. Both types are favored for their robustness, long lifespan, and resistance to fouling.</p></p>
