<p><p>BGA inspection systems encompass various market types, including electrical testing, optical or visual inspection, and X-ray inspection. Electrical testing assesses the functionality and connectivity of BGA connections, ensuring reliable performance. Optical inspection employs cameras to visually check for defects, while enhanced systems utilize advanced imaging for detailed analysis. X-ray inspection provides insight into hidden solder joints and internal structures, enabling the detection of faults that are not visible externally. Each method plays a vital role in ensuring quality and reliability in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
