<p><ul><li>Proton Exchange Membranes</li><li>Ion Exchange Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water electrolysis market features various membrane types that facilitate the electrochemical process. Proton exchange membranes (PEMs) are key for their efficiency in conducting protons while acting as a barrier to gases, making them ideal for compact, high-performance systems. In contrast, ion exchange membranes allow for the selective transfer of ions, providing versatility in different applications, including alkaline electrolysis. Together, these membranes play a critical role in enhancing the efficiency and sustainability of hydrogen production through electrolysis.</p></p>
