<p><p>The CNC laser welding machine market is categorized into three main types: fiber lasers, solid-state lasers, and CO2 lasers. Fiber lasers use optical fibers to generate laser light, offering high efficiency and precision for various materials. Solid-state lasers rely on solid gain mediums, providing powerful and stable beams suitable for industrial applications. CO2 lasers utilize gas as the medium, excelling in cutting thicker materials. Each type serves specific welding needs, with benefits that cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
