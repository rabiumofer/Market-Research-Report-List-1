<p><p>The dyeing auxiliaries market includes various types of chemicals that enhance textile dyeing processes. **Dispersing agents** stabilize dye particles, ensuring uniform color distribution. **Sequestering agents** bind metal ions, preventing impurities from affecting dye quality. **Scouring agents** clean fabrics by removing oils, waxes, and dirt, preparing them for dyeing. **Wetting agents** reduce surface tension, allowing dyes to penetrate fibers evenly. **Other auxiliaries** include leveling agents, fixing agents, and softeners, each playing a role in improving dye efficiency, fabric quality, and colorfastness.</p></p>
