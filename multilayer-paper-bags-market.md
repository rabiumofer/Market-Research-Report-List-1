<p><p>The multilayer paper bags market includes various types based on construction and functionality. Sewn open mouth bags feature durable stitching for secure closure, ideal for bulk goods. Pasted open mouth bags are sealed with adhesive and commonly used for various retail applications. Pasted valve bags incorporate a valve for easy filling and efficient material handling. Pinch bottom bags provide a secure closure through pinching at the bottom, suitable for powders. Self-opening satchels are designed for convenience and quick access, often employed in food packaging.</p></p>
