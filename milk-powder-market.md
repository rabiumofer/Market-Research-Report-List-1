<p><ul><li>Skim Milk Powder</li><li>Whole Milk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milk powder market primarily consists of Skim Milk Powder (SMP) and Whole Milk Powder (WMP). SMP is produced by removing most of the fat content from milk, resulting in a low-fat product ideal for use in baking, dairy products, and infant formula. In contrast, WMP retains the full fat content, offering a creamier texture and richer flavor, making it popular for culinary applications and as a staple in various dairy products. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
