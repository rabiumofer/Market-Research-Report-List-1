<p><p>The PTFE films market includes hydrophobic, hydrophilic, and other specialized films. Hydrophobic PTFE films repel water, making them ideal for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as filtration and electrical insulation. Hydrophilic PTFE films, on the other hand, attract water, enhancing wettability and liquid absorption, commonly used in medical and filtration industries. Other PTFE films cater to niche applications, offering unique properties like enhanced durability, chemical resistance, or thermal stability, tailored for specific industrial or technological needs. Each type serves distinct purposes across diverse sectors.</p></p>
